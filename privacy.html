Privacy Policy for CampuStay

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

Welcome to CampuStay. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you authenticate, we collect your email address. This authentication is securely handled via Google Firebase Auth.
  • Profile Data: Name, age, university, year of study, gender, budget preferences, and profile photos that you choose to upload to complete your profile.
  • Preference & Usage Data: Your answers to the Compatibility Quiz (used solely to calculate roommate matching percentages) and your swiping history (likes/dislikes on profiles and apartments).
  • Communication Data: The content of messages sent within the chat rooms established between matched users or hosts.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to deliver the core functionality of CampuStay:

  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To process roommate compatibility scores based on quiz responses.
  • To enable apartment listings, liking systems, and secure direct messaging.

3. Third-Party Data Processors

To provide a seamless experience, we share data with trusted infrastructure providers:

  • Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore: For real-time database hosting, secure data storage, and authentication.
  • Expo Services: For handling application development runtime and secure push notification routing.

4. Data Retention & The "Right to be Forgotten" (GDPR)

We respect your right to control your personal data.

  • Account Deletion: You can execute the "Delete Account" action from your profile settings at any time. This permanently and instantly purges your record from our system, including your profile, quiz responses, apartment likes, and swipe logs.
  • Chat Continuity: For partner safety and continuity, sent chat messages will remain visible to your conversation partners but will be completely anonymized under the title "Noone (Deleted account)".

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at: support@campustay.com
